Cashless Transactions with KindTap

We’ve simplified the payment process with KindTap – a fully digital, secure, cardless way to purchase our product. No ATM’s, and no cards; with KindTap you have the option to “Pay Now” through a streamlined bank account connection, or “Pay Later” with a revolving line of credit – all through a secure app on your mobile device.

Safe and Secure

This solution eliminates the need to carry cash as well as ensures a secure connection between the merchant and your bank.

No ACH Fees

No more ATM fees! When you link your bank account and make a purchase you pay no fees for that transaction.
